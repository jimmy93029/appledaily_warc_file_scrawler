About News Archive

News Archive is dedicated to preserving and organizing historical news articles.

Why archive news? Here are some reasons:

  • News articles provide a valuable historical record of events.
  • By archiving news, we ensure access to important information over time.
  • Having a structured and searchable news archive makes it easier to find past events.

Our goal is to continuously add new articles to this archive, making them easily accessible and searchable.

The source code for this website is available on GitHub. The code is refers to Niche museum which is designed by Simon Willison here.
